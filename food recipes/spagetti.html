Spaghetti Recipe

Description of the food

This is a spagetti recipe that mainly focuses on the original parts. There are no extra things just sauce, meat, and noodles. This is something I enojoyed as a kid ever since I was young

ingredients

  • ground beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • basil
  • pasta
  • tomato sauce

Steps to make spagetti

  1. brown the beef in a large pan
  2. throw in the spices listed above, salt, pepper, basil, and tomato sauce
  3. after cooking the meat with the spice and sauces cook the noodles and add it to the sauce when its done
