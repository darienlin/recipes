Spam Fried Rice

Ingredients Needed

  • Spam
  • Rice
  • salt and pepper
  • soy sauce
  • egg

Steps

  1. first fry the spam and the egg
  2. after both items are cooked put the rice in and add the seasoning
  3. you can also sad soy sauce if it is not salty enough

Spam fried rice was something I ate a lot when I was a kid when there were leftovers and I wanted to make something but couldn't get more ingredients.
